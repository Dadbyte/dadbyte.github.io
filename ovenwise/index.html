Reminders that respect you

Every buzz is a real kitchen visit.

Signal, not noise

Steps are consolidated so every alert is worth standing up for — and you can trim them further: first fold only, prep steps off. One separate loud channel is reserved for the alert that can't wait: "Take it out!"

Follow along, your way

A bake-along guide with swipeable step cards — times, gram amounts, and what to look for. Tap any reminder to open it right at that step. Prefer your calendar? Export every step as a calendar event (.ics).

Preheat to cooling rack

A live bake-window timer keeps watch at the end: countdown strip, full-screen ring timer, and a pinned notification until the loaf is safely out.

Fermentation steps say "your dough should look noticeably risen — shape now" — because dough doesn't read clocks.
