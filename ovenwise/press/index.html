About Ovenwise

Tell Ovenwise when you want to bake. It plans everything backward from that moment, down to the clock time of every step: when to build your levain, mix, stretch and fold, shape, cold-retard, and preheat. It works the active steps around your sleep, scores every schedule honestly (Great / Good / Tight / Difficult), covers 17 baking styles with their own fermentation science, and reminds you on time with a bake-along guide, a live bake timer, and calendar export. There's no account, no ads, and no analytics. Everything lives on your phone.
